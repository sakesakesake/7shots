Drink Sake

There are two ways to drink Sake.
It is either "cold" or "hot".
I personally prefer cold sake in summer days, and hot sake for winter days. Normally expensive sake is for cold.

Hot Sake

Hot sake is approximately 42F.
1, Pour sake into decanter.
2, Boil water in a saucepan.
3, Turn off the heat and gently lower the container of sake into the water.
Well, if it is too much work, microwave for 30 to 60 seconds and that will do too.

Where can I find sake?

Normally, asian supermarket have some types of sake.
Sometimes asian restaurants serve sake too. The tricky part is a lot of people mistake Korean Soju for Japanese sake. They are completely different alcohol. Sake doesn't have fruit flavors so if the drink you got have nice strawberry flavors, they are not sake.
